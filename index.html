MY WORK

  • Poller

    Poller is a polling website where users can create polls to be voted on by other users or guests!

    The homepage's poll tiles constantly change based on trending/popular polls and appear larger when they are even more popular than the rest of the trending tiles!

    This website uses Masonry (a grid system), Bootstrap, Sass, and Pug Jade on the front-end.

    On the back, I used Node/Express and a NoSQL MongoDB to do all data handling server-sided before rendering the Pug to the client to create a responsive ux.

    Biggest lessons learned?... On larger complex projects where you may have multiple controllers handling different processes on the back, call backs are an extremely powerful tool to maintain async! Also, start every project with a long term organized plan and create controllers/handlers first- dont do logic/rendering/database connections in your routing file! Its just messy and confusing to others!
